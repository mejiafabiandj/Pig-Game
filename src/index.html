Rules

Roll the dice and sum both values to your score, first player to get 100 Points Wins!

After every roll you can choose between roll again or to “hold” (it becomes next player's turn).

Either die rolls 1

If rolls a 1 on either die, they add nothing to your total score and it becomes the next player’s turn

Snake Eyes! BOTH dice show 1

Your total score goes to 0, and your turn ends... Good luck next time

Doubles (Except snake eyes)

The sum of the dice rolled is added to the turn total, as with any roll, and you must roll again
